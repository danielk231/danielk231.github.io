Camping Essentials

Here are some essential items to bring on your camping trip:

  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Camping stove
  • Flashlight
  • First aid kit
  • Water bottle
  • Map and compass
  • Food and snacks

Camping Safety Tips

Ensure a safe camping trip with these tips:

  • Choose a safe campsite away from hazards.
  • Set up your tent properly to avoid accidents.
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun.
  • Keep food sealed to prevent attracting animals.
  • Follow campground rules and regulations.
  • Be prepared for emergencies with a first aid kit.
